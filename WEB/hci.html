Human Computer Interaction

Assignment

Over the last years, Groenrijk has become more than just a garden center. They incorporated indoor decoration to their existing selection of plants, supplies and outdoor styling. Besides Spring - the time when gardens are blossoming again - Christmas is now also considered one of the most important periods of the year. To complement the Christmas market in stores, Groenrijk requested to launch a promotional website where consumers and SMEs can design and order fully decorated Christmas trees. The assignment is as following: design the promotional Christmas tree website.

Project: HCI

Date: November 20, 2017

Target group: People looking for a Christmas tree
